Church Testimony

As members of the Guadalupe Baptist Church, we proudly share our collective testimony with the world. From the day of our establishment, our church has encountered numerous challenges, yet our faith community remains steadfast, founded on the unshakable rock of our beliefs. Throughout the trials posed by the pandemic, we persevered in our commitment to worship and ministry without wavering. In the midst of suffering, our unwavering faith in Jesus sustained us, and in return, we've been abundantly blessed—a testament to the enduring strength of our shared devotion
